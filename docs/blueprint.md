# **App Name**: VogueQuant

## Core Features:

- Fashion Item Catalog: Users can add and manage detailed information for various fashion items, including type, material, seasonality, cost, and selling price.
- Historical Sales & Forecast Input: Allow users to manually input historical sales data or future sales forecasts per item or category to inform quantity planning.
- Core Quantity Calculation: Calculate baseline suggested production or order quantities based on user-provided historical data and predefined business rules.
- AI Trend Adjustment Tool: An AI tool that analyzes item attributes and general fashion concepts to suggest informed adjustments to initial quantity plans, considering potential market trends or item characteristics.
- Quantity Plan Overview: Display a clear overview of the planned quantities per item, allowing for manual overrides and showing estimated profit projections.
- AI-Powered Stock Level Suggestion: Suggest optimal stock levels by calling the Gemini API to analyze weekly sales data and predict future demand.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional blue-grey (`#2E4A75`) to evoke sophistication and precision, reflecting careful planning and data analysis in the fashion industry. This color provides a strong yet understated foundation for key elements.
- Background color: Dark theme, inspired by Apple Intelligence, with a focus on readability and a modern, sophisticated aesthetic.
- Accent color: A vibrant, fresh green (`#39B86B`) is used to highlight calls to action, important data points, and positive indicators. Its brightness and saturation provide an energetic contrast while complementing the cooler primary tones.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (humanist sans-serif) for its elegant, fashion-aligned personality and art-and-design flair. Body text font: 'Inter' (grotesque-style sans-serif) for exceptional legibility and a modern, objective feel, ensuring clear presentation of data and details.
- Clean, structured layouts with ample white space to enhance readability and focus on data points and item visuals. Grid-based systems will be employed for consistency and efficient organization of inventory and planning information.
- Subtle and quick micro-interactions for feedback on data input and plan adjustments. Smooth transitions when navigating between item categories or viewing different planning reports, providing a polished user experience without being distracting.
